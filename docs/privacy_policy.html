Kotobi Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2, 2026

Developer: Xiaofeng Lei

1. Overview

Kotobi is an AI-powered Japanese learning app. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Summary: Kotobi stores your study data locally on your device. It does not operate its own user-data servers, does not require an account, and does not sell or share personal data, except when you explicitly use AI features that send content to the AI provider you configure.

2. Data We Collect and How We Use It

2.1 Data stored only on your device

Data Purpose Stored
Imported PDF and audio files Source material for sentence extraction and study On-device only
Extracted sentences and study cards Learning content On-device database
Study progress and review history Spaced repetition scheduling and progress tracking On-device database
Audio recordings you make Speaking practice and transcription on request On-device; deleted after transcription workflow
AI API keys Authenticate requests to the AI provider you choose Stored locally on device
App settings and preferences Language, provider choice, notifications, and study settings On-device only

2.2 Data sent to third-party AI services

When you use AI features, the following data is sent directly to the AI provider whose API key or server you configure. Kotobi does not operate a server that proxies or stores this data.

Feature Data sent Recipient
Card generation Japanese sentence text and related prompt content OpenAI or DeepSeek, depending on user configuration
Speaking practice Audio recording and generated transcript/feedback prompt data Configured AI provider
AI connection test Short test request Configured AI provider

Provider privacy policies:

  • OpenAI: openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
  • DeepSeek: platform.deepseek.com/downloads/DeepSeek%20Privacy%20Policy.pdf

2.3 Local AI server

If you configure a local AI server on your own network, data is sent only to that server.

3. Permissions

Permission / Access Why it is needed
Microphone To record your voice for speaking practice
Internet To reach AI APIs and optional local AI server endpoints
Local Network To connect to a local AI server and support Wi-Fi file transfer
Notifications To send scheduled study reminders when enabled by the user

4. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require user accounts.
  • We do not run analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not store study data on Kotobi-operated servers.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains on your device unless you send content to an AI provider you configured. You can delete data by removing books or cards in the app, deleting app data, or uninstalling the app.

6. Children’s Privacy

Kotobi is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, the effective date on this page will be updated.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact xiaofeng.lei.bj@gmail.com.

9. App Scope

This policy applies to Kotobi on iPhone / iOS and may also be used for the same app on other supported platforms where the same privacy practices apply.
